ANTONIO BIO
He made his album debut at the age of 16, in late 2005 with the self-titled album Chris Brown. The album included the single "Run It!", which topped the Billboard Hot 100, making the singer the first artist male to have the first single from his debut album at number 1, only since Montell Jordan in 1995. The album has sold more than two million copies in the United States and was certified double platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA).
